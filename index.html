About Us


Welcome to Donate’n’Find, a non-profit organization located in Queensland Australia, providing service for people donating and/or seeking donated items. Offering a possession no longer needed/or used to gratify someone else’s need is a GREAT feeling. Sports equipment or clothing your children outgrew, kitchen appliances you don’t use anymore or collectibles you want to pass along. If you think about it, there are likely many things in your possession no use to you, but that would totally make someone else’s day!


There are many websites to sell items, but the preparation of setting up an account, calculating fair prices, as well as shipping logistics is overwhelming. All this effort rarely pays off, even more so because the items are used. That is why donating makes more sense. People are always looking for something for nothing! (or very little). This is the true essence of generosity!


That’s why we created Donate’n’Find; to pay forward the spirit of giving and receiving. Our landing page will provide a no-hassle process for donating and finding items, to make the process uncomplicated for the consumer. The goal is to provide an intermediary service for those who want to donate or seek gently used manageable items for feasible exchange. We will offer consumers an opportunity to connect with others to gratify their generosity, pass along no longer needed items and offer a practical solution exchange platform.
