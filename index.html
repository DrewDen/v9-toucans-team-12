Donate n Find

About Us

Welcome to Donate n Find, our online non-profit organization providing a platform for people donating and/or seeking items available for donation. Offering a possession no longer needed/or used to gratify someone else's need is a GREAT feeling. Sports equipment or clothing your children outgrew, kitchen appliances you don’t use anymore or collectibles you want to pass along. If you think about it, there are likely many things in your possession no use to you, but that would totally make someone else's day!

There are many places online to sell items, but the preparation of setting up an account, calculating fair prices, as well as shipping logistics is overwhelming. All this effort rarely pays off, even more so because the items are used. That is why donating makes more sense. People are always looking for something for nothing! (or very little). This is the true essence of generosity!

That’s why we created Donate n Find; to pay forward the spirit of giving and receiving. Our site provides a no-hassle process for donating and finding items, to make the process uncomplicated for you. Our goal is to provide an intermediary service for those who want to donate or seek gently used manageable items for feasible exchange. We provide you with the opportunity to connect with others to gratify their generosity, pass along no longer needed items and offer a practical solution exchange platform.

How it works

Donate n Find isn’t a fancy platform with several moving parts. We provide a practical solution and our simple design and innovative idea speak. Two distinct options are readily available to you. We make it simple for you to either make a donation, locate one or do both.

  When our Donate button is clicked, a form is popped up to be filled out requesting basic information about you and an item you wish to donate. When finished, your form can be submitted and the information is sent and stored. We only require you to provide minimal contact information with a brief description about an item you wish to donate.

  When our Find button is clicked, a list of items is made visible. Our list contain pictures and a brief description of available items. Additionally, giving you the option to send an email to the person making the donation. As the person seeking a donation, you are free to contact the donor privately. With the process in motion, the current screen will close.
